On the day we met, with playful jest,
Who knew our hearts would be put to the test?
From roasting words to love so deep,
In each other's arms, we'd find our keep.

Through every fight, we stood our ground,
In love’s embrace, we're always bound.
With every tear and every laugh,
We’ve walked together, shared the path.

The hurdles came, but we didn’t fall,
We faced them head-on, standing tall.
Hand in hand, we’ve won each fight,
Together we’ve made the darkness bright.

Eight months have passed, and here we stand,
Stronger together, hand in hand.
Through ups and downs, we’ve found our way,
In your love, I’ll forever stay.

Our journey’s just begun, it’s true,
But I’m so blessed to be with you.
Through every storm, and every cheer,
I’ll love you more, year after year.

So here’s to us, my love, my friend,
Together forever, till the very end.
Eight months of love, of joy, and grace,
With you, my love, I’ve found my place.
